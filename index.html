Since 2018, our flagship fund, Cambrian Systematic Strategies, has successfully earned our investors exceptional, uncorrelated absolute returns from exposure to digital assets while limiting downside risks.

FUND PERFORMANCE

Value of $1 million invested since November 2018

In October 2021, we launched two of the only actively managed, algorithmically traded, Ethereum and Bitcoin specific trusts, with both significantly outperforming their benchmarks to date.

2017

Founders Jay Posner and Martin Green, with their combined decades of hedge fund experience and tech knowledge, begin work on a systematic, algorithmic and data-driven approach to digital assets.
Cambrian is born in Mill Valley, California, just north of the Golden Gate Bridge.

2018-19

From November 2018 through June 2019, the founders conduct 'pre-fund' trading with proprietary capital, while honing their algorithmic approach.

2019

The Cambrian Systematic Strategies (CSS) fund, trading in select crypto assets, is launched on July 1, 2019 with partner capital.
The fund ends 2019 with a 78.5% return, net of fees.

2020

Tony Fenner-Leitão, an early investor and advisor to the firm, with over 30 years of experience in the financial sector including eight as a senior member of globally recognized systematic investment firms, joins as the President.
Daniel Tyreus, with a wide technical background including data engineering at scale and over 20 years of experience in leading engineering teams at multiple venture-backed startups, takes charge as the firm’s Head of Engineering.
C-Corp Conversion and Equity Raise.
The fund ends 2020 with a 168.1% return, net of fee.

2021

Cambrian launches two single-investment vehicles - Cambrian Bitcoin Systematic Trust and Cambrian Ethereum Systematic Trust - based on a similar investment approach as its flagship fund.
Double the firm's AUM to $200M from 03/31/2021 to 08/31/2021.

2022

Cambrian's risk-management navigates the cratering of crypto values, resulting in July YTD positive returns, net of fees, for CSS and the Cambrian Ethereum Trust.
Despite volatility in the crypto market the trusts protect capital YTD. The Ethereum trust even generates positive returns.
